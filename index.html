Basic Cat Facts

  • Cats sleep about 12 to 16 hours every day.
  • Kittens are born with closed eyes.
  • Cats can rotate their ears almost 180 degrees.
  • A group of kittens is called a kindle.
  • Cats use whiskers to help sense spaces around them.
